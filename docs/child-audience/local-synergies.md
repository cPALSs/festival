# Theme dressing & Greater Sacramento comps

Without Disney IP, **theme** is how disparate Sacramento vendors read as one world.

---

## Three-layer model

| Layer | What | Example (MAF) |
|-------|------|---------------|
| **1 — Local asset** | Booked org or city pattern | Library storytime · magic act |
| **2 — Festival skin** | Costume, script, décor, music | Moon rabbit narrator · harvest palette |
| **3 — Ritual handoff** | Object or action that goes home | Lantern · moon story card |

Layer 1 works without Layer 2; Layer 2 makes it **ours**; Layer 3 makes it **sticky**.

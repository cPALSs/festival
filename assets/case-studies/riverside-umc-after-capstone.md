## Context

Riverside United Methodist Church runs a **neighborhood Mid-Autumn fair** (2025: Sep 27, Vallejo Way, Sacramento) — game booths, food, local performers, parish lantern parade. Lane B by category; **R0 warm-up** by role.

## What happened

2025 fair landed **after** lunar weekend capstone window. Coalition landscape tagged “direct comp”; field read is **parish + surrounding blocks**, not metro attendance fight.

## Lesson

**Small warm-up hosts need portable identity, not coalition merge.** Parish cohesion > cPALSs branding. Best help: **supplier lists** + **pickup-haulable centerpiece lantern** → enter at **capstone lantern contest** (featured in dusk procession). See [Capstone Lantern Contest program](../../../../Projects%20-%20Mid-Autumn%20Festival/2026/Program%20&%20Entertainment/Capstone%20Lantern%20Contest%20-%20Roll-up%20Program.md).

## What to do differently

- Outreach tone: warm-up, not competitor
- Offer centerpiece lantern guidance (pickup-scale) + capstone guest slot — don’t program their fair
- Catalog supplier lists for parish-scale hosts

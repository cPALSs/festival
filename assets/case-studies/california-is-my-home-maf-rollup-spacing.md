## Context

Elk Grove runs two Lane A Mid-Autumn producers: **California Is My Home** @ District 56 (one-night fair, ~2–3K) and **cPALSs/VACOS** rebuild @ Derr-Okamoto (lunar weekend, 1.5-day). CAIMH packs the Avenue stage; District 56 campus — especially **The Preserve** — is mostly idle during their fair.

## What happened

2025: CAIMH Sep 12 @ District 56 (70+ vendors, KCRA). cPALSs MAF not at full capstone scale in EG same cycle. Feb 2026 LNY: parallel events (CAIMH @ District 56, cPALSs @ Regional Park) — merge not attempted.

## Lesson

**Coordinate by default; merge only at campus scale.** Guest slot on CAIMH’s main stage fails on space and heritage programming ownership. Viable co-production looks like **Illumination-tier** multi-zone District 56 (Avenue + senior-center Stage 2 + Preserve procession loop) — not one-night fair absorption.

## What to do differently

- 2026: Run Derr-Okamoto if staffed; coordinate dates/cross-promo with Eva Zhou
- Horizon: Explore unified Elk Grove MAF vision with CAIMH before assuming collision
- CCSD: Derr-Okamoto is CSD; District 56 is City — GPF remains CCSD’s fall anchor
